Our school background

Light Academy schools (LAS) is a well-established, privately owened co-educational schools. The school is suitated in the beautiful lowland at Lweza-Kigo Road 4km off Entebbe Road, easily accessible and perfectly conducive environment for learning, free of Noise and other extraneous distractions associated with urban and peri-urban settings of the City. The school is registered with the Ministry of Education and Sports to implement the Uganda National Curriculum and prepare students,the Ordinary Level Uganda Certificate of Education(UCE) and the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE).
